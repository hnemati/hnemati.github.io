About Me

I'm a researcher at the Center for IT-Security, Privacy and Accountability (CISPA), working in the Secure & Privacy-friendly Information Processing group lead by Prof. Michael Backes. My research interests lie at the intersection of formal methods and computer security. Specifically, I am interested in Operating System security, formal modeling and verification of systems, virtualization technology, interactive theorem proving and machine code analysis. Prior to joining CISPA I was a PhD student in the Theoretical Computer Science group (TCS) at the School of Computer Science and Communication at Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) under Prof. Mads Dam.
