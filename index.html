About Me

I am a Research Group Leader at the Helmholtz Center for Information Security (CISPA). Prior to this, I was a Post-Doctoral researcher at CISPA working with Prof. Michael Backes. I did my PhD in computer science at the School of Computer Science and Communication at Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) under the supervision of Prof. Mads Dam. My research interests include security of systems software, formal methods and program logics, interactive theorem proving and machine code analysis.
